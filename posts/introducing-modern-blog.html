Introducing Modern Blog

Aug 15, 2025 · 5 min read

Welcome to Modern Blog — a minimal, responsive starter built with just HTML, CSS, and a sprinkle of JavaScript. It focuses on great typography, generous spacing, and accessible interactions so your words shine on any device.

Features

  • Responsive layout with a flexible grid
  • Clean, readable typography with sensible defaults
  • Mobile navigation with good accessibility
  • Light and dark mode via the user's system preference

Getting around

The homepage lists recent posts as cards. Tap or click any card to read the full article. On small screens, use the menu button to open the navigation.

Code block example

<article class="prose">
  <h1>Your Title</h1>
  <p>Write your story here.</p>
</article>
Good design is as little design as possible — Dieter Rams
